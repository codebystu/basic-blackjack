How to play


The objective of the game is to get a score closer to 21 than the dealer, without going over 21.


You have a starting bankroll of £100


Choose your stake by clicking on the £1 £2 and £5 chip symbols,
multiple clicks will add to your stake,
the clear button will clear your stake back to 0

When happy with your stake, hit the deal button


You are dealt 2 cards face up, the dealer is dealt 1 card face up and 1 face down

To get another card click the Hit button, if you are happy with your hand click Stand

If you go over 21 you have Bust and lose your stake immediately


The dealer will then take their turn, dealer hits on 16 and stands on 17


If you win you get your stake returned plus the same again

If you get 21 in your first 2 cards you have Blackjack and are paid 3 x winnings


Game ends when your bankroll gets to £0



The house always wins eventually....
